Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Nik Mohamad Arif Farhan, AS Tech Legacy began as a humble repair counter in Terengganu and evolved into a national tech partner.

Origins

Nik Mohd Arif Farhan launched AS Tech Legacy to provide fast, affordable, and high-quality IT services for his community. From the first motherboard repairs, the mission was clear: reliable service backed by technical expertise.

Where We Are Today

By 2025 we operate a dedicated outlet in Kuala Terengganu, serve 10,000+ customers, and are recognised for laptop, PC, and motherboard mastery, COD delivery, and TikTok Live engagements.
